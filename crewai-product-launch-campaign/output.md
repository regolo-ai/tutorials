# Premium Organic Face Serum Launch Campaign Plan

## Campaign Overview
**Product:** Premium Organic Face Serum
**Target Market:** Eco-conscious millennials (25-40) with $50-100 monthly skincare budget
**Campaign Duration:** 30 days
**Expected ROAS:** 3x
**Total Budget:** $150,000

## Channel Mix and Budget Allocation

### Paid Media (65% - $97,500)
- **Facebook/Instagram Ads:** 40% - $39,000
  - 5 ad variations (price gap, science-backed, sustainable beauty, mid-tier gap, results)
  - 25% budget for retargeting
  - 75% budget for new audience acquisition
- **Google Shopping Ads:** 15% - $14,625
  - Product listing ads with competitive pricing
  - Search term targeting for organic skincare
- **YouTube Pre-roll Ads:** 10% - $9,750
  - 30-second educational videos about ingredient benefits
- **TikTok Ads:** 10% - $9,750
  - 15-second before/after transformation videos
  - Influencer collaboration content
- **Affiliate Marketing:** 10% - $9,750
  - Skincare influencers and beauty bloggers

### Organic Media (20% - $30,000)
- **Content Marketing:** 10% - $15,000
  - Blog posts about organic skincare benefits
  - Ingredient spotlight series
- **Email Marketing:** 5% - $7,500
  - 3-email sequence with automation
- **PR & Partnerships:** 5% - $7,500
  - Beauty editor outreach
  - Sustainable lifestyle influencer partnerships

### Direct (15% - $22,500)
- **Landing Page Optimization:** 5% - $7,500
- **Product Photography & UX:** 5% - $7,500
- **Customer Support & Onboarding:** 5% - $7,500

## Week-by-Week Timeline

### Week 1 (Days 1-7): Teaser & Pre-Launch
**Day 1-2: Email Teaser Campaign**
- Send teaser email to existing list
- Create social media countdown posts
- Launch landing page with "Coming Soon" messaging

**Day 3-4: Influencer Outreach**
- Contact 20 micro-influencers (10K-50K followers)
- Send product samples for review
- Schedule content calendar for launch week

**Day 5-7: Paid Media Pre-Launch**
- Launch Facebook/Instagram teaser ads
- Begin Google Shopping ad setup
- Start TikTok content creation

### Week 2 (Days 8-14): Launch Week
**Day 8-9: Official Launch**
- Launch full email sequence (teaser, launch, follow-up)
- Push all paid advertising campaigns live
- Begin influencer content rollout

**Day 10-12: Content Marketing**
- Publish 3 blog posts about organic skincare benefits
- Launch ingredient spotlight series
- Create educational videos for YouTube

**Day 13-14: Retargeting Campaigns**
- Launch retargeting ads for website visitors
- Create lookalike audiences from initial conversions
- Optimize ad copy based on early performance

### Week 3 (Days 15-21): Growth Phase
**Day 15-17: A/B Testing**
- Test 3 different landing page variations
- Test 5 different ad copy variations
- Test different CTA buttons and visual elements

**Day 18-20: Paid Media Optimization**
- Increase budget for high-performing channels
- Pause underperforming ad variations
- Scale successful influencer partnerships

**Day 21: Mid-Campaign Review**
- Analyze performance metrics
- Adjust budget allocation based on ROI
- Prepare for holiday season upsell

### Week 4 (Days 22-30): Conversion & Retention
**Day 22-24: Conversion Optimization**
- Implement upsell and cross-sell strategies
- Launch retargeting campaigns for abandoned carts
- Create urgency with limited-time offers

**Day 25-27: Customer Success Stories**
- Share customer testimonials and reviews
- Launch user-generated content campaign
- Create before/after transformation content

**Day 28-30: Final Push**
- Intensify retargeting campaigns
- Launch final promotional offers
- Prepare for next product launch planning
